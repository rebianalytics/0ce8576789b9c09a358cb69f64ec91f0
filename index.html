Who We Are

We pride ourselves on being pragmatic in our solution recommendations keeping in mind organizations have often spent large amounts on their legacy operational and modeling systems. As such, our first line of attack is to leverage the existing technology and infrastructure and integrate our platform to provide the desired new functionality.

We have successfully delivered integrated BI & PM solutions with JD Edwards, MRI, YARDI, PROMAN, and Finance One. Our solution platform integrates with popular COTS modeling packages (Argus, Cougar, and Estate Master) and can blend these outputs with data from ERP and other operational systems.

The REBI Analytics team are experts in the end-to-end design, build and implementation of business intelligence solutions in the Real Estate and Construction Professional Services.

Our consultants have deep experience in both the subject matter and operational considerations that underpin the success of enterprise BI implementations. Backed up with formal qualifications in Accounting, Land Economics, Information Technology, and Engineering, we are well credentialed to tackle the most complex issues arising in all phases of project delivery lifecycle.
